about=EigenBand brings classical machine learning dimensionality reduction directly into the QGIS canvas. It flattens multi-spectral rasters (like Sentinel-2 or Landsat), applies Principal Component Analysis (PCA) via scikit-learn, and automatically generates and styles a new 3-band RGB raster based on the top principal components. Perfect for geological mapping, vegetation analysis, and feature extraction.
